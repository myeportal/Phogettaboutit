# PHOITALIA Brand Identity Guide

> **Brand architecture guardrail:** Treat **PHOITALIA** as the primary guest-facing hospitality brand. Treat **phogettaboutit.com** as a domain and marketing asset. Treat **PHOGETTABOUTIT** only as a secondary creative expression.

## Brand Idea in One Line
**Vietnam at lunch. Italy at dinner. One memorable house.**

## Direction
PHOITALIA should feel like one well-run restaurant with two emotional tempos connected by a map-like route across the day.

## Identity Notes
- Lunch is clearly Vietnam.
- Dinner is clearly Italy.
- Use subtle route or world-map cues only as supporting storytelling.
- Treat “the i side has 2 faces” as a future logo exploration note, not literal launch artwork.

## Guardrails
- PHOITALIA leads.
- phogettaboutit.com supports.
- PHOGETTABOUTIT stays secondary.
- Never frame the concept as novelty-first fusion.
