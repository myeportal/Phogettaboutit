# 17 Website Copy — PHOITALIA / Phogettaboutit LLC

> **Brand architecture note:** This website copy assumes **PHOITALIA** is the primary guest-facing restaurant mark. **phogettaboutit.com** may remain the traffic-capture domain and `PHOGETTABOUTIT` may appear only as a clearly secondary campaign or creative expression.

## Website Goal
Create a site that explains the concept in seconds, turns curiosity into action, and supports both lunch conversion and dinner reservation intent.

---

### Primary Headline Option

### Core Supporting Line
A Wyoming restaurant with two clear dayparts: bright Vietnamese lunch from 10:30 AM to 2:30 PM and warm Italia dinner from 4:30 PM to 9:30 PM.

## UX Notes for the Website Team
- Keep daypart CTAs visible above the fold.
- Always show the descriptor line “Vietnamese Lunch • Italia Dinner.”
- Use separate lunch and dinner photography rather than mixed collage clutter.
- Consider a subtle global-map or route-line storytelling device to reinforce Vietnam-to-Italy movement.
- Accessibility, hours, ordering, and reservation paths should be easy to find within one tap or click.
