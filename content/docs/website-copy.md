# 17 Website Copy — PHOITALIA / Phogettaboutit LLC

> **Brand architecture note:** This website copy assumes **PHOITALIA** is the primary guest-facing restaurant mark. **phogettaboutit.com** may remain the traffic-capture domain and `PHOGETTABOUTIT` may appear only as a clearly secondary campaign or creative expression.

## Website Goal
Create a site that explains the concept in seconds, turns curiosity into action, and supports both lunch conversion and dinner reservation intent.

## Core Story
**Vietnam at Lunch. Italy at Dinner.**

PHOITALIA should read like one premium house with a clear route across the day: bright Vietnamese lunch from 10:30 AM to 2:30 PM, then warm Italia dinner from 4:30 PM to 9:30 PM.

## Homepage Direction
- Hero headline: **Vietnam at Lunch. Italy at Dinner.**
- Support line: lunch starts in Vietnam with herbs, broth, and speed; dinner lands in Italy with pasta, cocktails, and a warmer room.
- Preserve the descriptor line: **Vietnamese Lunch • Italia Dinner**.
- Keep CTAs separated by daypart.
- Use subtle route-line or world-map storytelling if the UI needs a strong visual concept.

## Brand Guardrails
- **PHOITALIA** leads all guest-facing touchpoints.
- **phogettaboutit.com** remains supporting only.
- **PHOGETTABOUTIT** remains secondary only.
- The concept is rhythm-based, not gimmick fusion.
